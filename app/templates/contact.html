About Us

The Laboratory of Intelli-manufactureing Food Engineering (LIFE) is a research group at the School of Food and Light Industry, Nanjing Tech University. Our team specializes in the exploration and development of biological resources from extremophilic microorganisms.

Our primary research focuses include:

  • Screening and isolation of radiation-resistant microorganisms from extreme environments
  • Genomic and functional analysis of extremophiles with unique biological properties
  • Development of computational tools for predicting radiation resistance in bacteria
  • Exploring potential applications of extremophilic microorganisms in biotechnology and food industry
  • Investigating the mechanisms of microbial adaptation to extreme conditions
